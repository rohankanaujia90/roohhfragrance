Our Story

Crafted With
Passion & Purpose

Roohh Fragrances was born from a deep love for the art of perfumery — a tradition that has been alive for centuries in the heart of the East. The word "Roohh" means soul in Arabic and Urdu, and every fragrance we craft carries a piece of that soul.

We source the finest raw materials — from Bulgarian rose petals to aged Assamese oud — and blend them by hand in small batches to ensure unparalleled quality and depth.
